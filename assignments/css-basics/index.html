Tortoise Facts

Tortoises are a popular pet for many people that want a companion that is cute, easy to take care of, quiet and will be with them forever. Tortoises can live for a very long time and can rage from 50 to 100 years.

Tortiose Diet

Tortiose's are herbivores and their diet consists of manily plan-based foods. These foods can range from fruits such as kiwis, strawberries and melons as well as vegetables such as carroes, pumpkins and cabbage.

Tortiose Habitat

There are a large amount of different breeds of Tortoises and they can be found in various different habitats. However, a vast majority of tortosies can be found in deserts, grasslands, mountainside, scrub forests and sand dunes.
