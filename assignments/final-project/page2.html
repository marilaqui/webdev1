Matcha Cake Roll

One of the most popular and unique flavors that have made its wat around the world is matcha. For those who love green tea, this one is for you. Many people have come up with different ways to use it instead of just putting it into drinks. One of my favorite is this matcha cake roll. Filled with a vanilla cream and rolled into a fluffy matcha cake is something that anyone would die for!

Strawberry Cream Cake

You can never go wrong with the classics! The strawberry cream cake is one of the most popular and well-known desserts in the world! Traditionally made in japan, the strawberry cream cake is something that many people would enjoy! This signiture cake has been seen in grocerie stores, bakeries and even in anime!

Simple Carrot Cake

Want to spet up your flavor profile? A simple carrot cake should do the trick! Commonly made during Easter or thanksgiving, carrot cake is one of those cakes that wouldn't think would be a cake at all. But suprisingly, a simple carrot cake is full of flavor, made with ingredients such as nutmet, cinnamon and even walnuts! Another cake to spice up your palette!
